Tabs limiter with queue settings

Tabs limit

Sets maximum of opened tabs. New tabs will be queued.
Pinned tabs and Chrome specific pages won't count for this limit, these are pages like chrome://newtab or chrome-extension://.
NOTE: "Reopen closed tab" will also queue it if the limit has been reached.

ICON



Sets the icon on the button.
JPG/PNG/ICO format and 19x19px is recommended or it will be resized and may not look good.
Maximum size of 100KB.
